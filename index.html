About Me.

I have graduated from Dhaka Medical College in Bangladesh then did basic and specialist surgical training at various centers in Bangladesh. I have received high quality vascular surgery training at major centers of excellence at Bangladesh Medical University and Dhaka Medical College Hospital. I underwent intensive emergency vascular training at the National Institute of Cardiovascular Diseases (NICVD), Dhaka—a critical component that significantly strengthened my ability to manage complex and life-threatening vascular emergencies with precision and confidence.

For the last 13+ years I have treated a large range of Vascular Diseases like Peripheral Arterial Occlusive Disease (PAOD), Venous Diseases, Vascular Anomalies, Vascular Access, Abdominal Aortic Aneurism, Thoracic Outlet Syndrome and also Vascular Trauma and Emergency Management. Specialist in Open Vascular Surgery, Endovascular Surgery and Laser Surgery. My work is grounded in scientific research and clinical outcomes.

I joined the Department of Vascular Surgery at Bangabandhu Sheikh Mujib Medical University in 2012 and continue at present as a Consultant of Vascular and Endovascular surgeon in Bangladesh Medical University.

With a calm demeanor, deep clinical insight, and unwavering dedication to ethical practice, Dr. Saha continues to stand out—not as a surgeon who always intervenes, but as one who knows when not to.
